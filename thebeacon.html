Introducing the Beacon

Always know if your net leaks.

Hit the Lights

Put the Beacons in your network and they warn if you have unexpected leaks. Spread them all around and show off how tight ship you are running.

Break out

Beacon emits test signals out of your network. Our service warns you if it detects changes in the ways they get out.

Physical

Send Beacon Appliances to remote sites, or leave behind in every meeting room you visit. Get alerts when people accidently connect your secure network to Internet.

Virtual

Beacon runs also in a Virtual Appliance you can launch inside Cloud-services and server hotels. Get a grip of the security of that third party service provider. Simply launch a Beacon for each segment and watch if it can call home.

Care Free

Just Launch It!

Spawn and Spread

Beacon does not require complex configuration. Order a physical appliance from our service or create unique virtual machines to be spread around. You just give labels to your Beacons so that you'll know where the alerts are coming from.

Replace

Update process is easy. You just swap the old Beacon with the new one. With the physical appliance you can just swap it's the SD-card.

Secure Architecture

Because security device demanding security exception is just silly.

One Way

The Beacon does not require inbound connections. You don't have to touch your access control in order to use them.

End-to-End Encrypted

Thanks to the Beacon's end-to-end encryption the receivers only know the ID's of your Beacons. The rest of the information is available only for you.

Your Disposable Heroes

Beacon lives in a read-only memory, which means you can dispose the mass media without worrying about leaks. Anything else would be just ironic, don't you think?
